How to cook up some eggs.

Here's how you cook a perfect sunny-side up:

  1. Prepare a small skillet and heat it up, medium-high heat.
  2. Pull out a tray of eggs.
  3. Once your pan is hot, sprinkle it with some olive oil.
  4. Have a trash can prepared next to where you are standing. You want it to be close to your stove.
  5. Take out as many eggs as you like, frying one egg at a time.
  6. Crack an egg, slide it into the pan and drop the shells carefully into the trash can
  7. Rip a sheet of foil about the size of your pan. Place it over the pan.
  8. Check the egg frequently. About halfway through frying, add water to your egg.
  9. Be sure to add salt to your egg just before it finishes frying.
  10. Once your egg is completly fried, you are ready to eat.
